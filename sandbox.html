What Is It?

LEARN BY DOING

The Sandbox is DDI's signature hands-on entrepreneurship experience. It's a space where you can experiment and start a real business—testing ideas without fear of failure. Business coaches and program mechanisms support and guide you through every challenge.

Think of it as a playground for entrepreneurs. You gain practical business experience through trial-and-error in a supported environment before entering the actual marketplace.
